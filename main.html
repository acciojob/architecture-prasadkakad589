Architecture is the art and science of designing and constructing buildings, blending function (safety, utility) with aesthetics (beauty, inspiration) to create spaces for living, working, and culture, encompassing diverse styles from ancient temples to modern skyscrapers and impacting society, heritage, and the environment. It involves planning, creating drawings, and building structures, serving practical needs like housing and hospitals while also expressing cultural identity, with aspects like information architecture focusing on organizing digital content for clarity.
